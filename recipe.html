Chocolate Chip Cookies

This classic chocolate chip cookies recipe is easy to make and results in soft , chewy, and delicious treat. Perfect for any occassion!

Ingredients

    1 cup of butter, softened 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
    • 2 teaspoon of vanilla extract 3 cups of all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups of chocolate chips

Directions

    Preheat oven to 350F (175C). Cream together butter, white sugar, brown sugar until smooth. Beat in one egg at a time, then stir in vanilla extract. In a separate bowl, mix flour,baking soda, and salt. Gradually blend dry ingredients into wet mixture. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop spoonfull of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet. bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden brown let cookies cool on wire rack before serving.
